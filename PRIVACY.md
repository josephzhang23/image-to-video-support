# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: February 20, 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the Photo to Video: AI Video Maker app ("Application") for iOS, created by Jiafu Zhang ("we", "us", or "our"). The Application is provided as a Freemium service.

## Information We Collect

When you use the Application, we may automatically collect:

*   Your device's IP address
*   Device type and operating system
*   App usage data (features used, time spent, session information)

We do not collect precise location data.

## User-Provided Content

The core functionality of the Application involves generating AI-powered videos from your photos, videos, audio, and text prompts. When you use these features, you provide:

*   **Images and photos** you select for video generation (which may contain human faces)
*   **Videos** you upload for enhancement or reference
*   **Audio files** you provide for avatar or talking video features
*   **Text prompts** describing the video you want to generate
*   **Technical parameters** such as resolution and duration settings

## How We Use Your Content

Your uploaded content is used solely to generate AI-powered videos as requested by you. This includes image-to-video generation, face-driven animation, avatar creation, image editing, and video enhancement.

Your content is **never** used for facial recognition, user identification, authentication, advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the video generation you initiate.

## Third-Party AI Processing

To generate videos, the Application transmits your uploaded content to the following third-party AI service providers for processing:

*   [FAL.ai](https://fal.ai/privacy) — AI video and image generation
*   [WaveSpeed AI](https://wavespeed.ai/static/privacy) — AI video generation

These providers process your content on their servers and return the generated videos to the Application.

The Application also uses the following third-party services:

*   [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) — Subscription and purchase management

## Face Data

Certain features involve photos containing human faces. We want to be clear about how this data is handled:

*   **Collection:** When you upload photos containing faces for video generation, avatar creation, or face-driven animation, those photos are transmitted to our AI service providers (FAL.ai and WaveSpeed AI). The Application does not perform local facial recognition, extract facial geometry, create facial feature maps, or generate biometric identifiers.
*   **Use:** Face data in your photos is used solely for AI video generation as requested by you. It is never used for facial recognition, identification, authentication, or advertising.
*   **Sharing:** Photos containing face data are sent only to FAL.ai and WaveSpeed AI for processing. No face data is sold or shared with any other third parties.
*   **No biometric data:** The Application does not collect, create, or store biometric identifiers or biometric information. No facial geometry, faceprint, or facial recognition template is extracted from your photos at any point.

## Data Storage and Retention

*   **Your photos:** The Application does not store your uploaded photos on its own servers. Photos are uploaded directly to the AI service providers for processing.
*   **AI provider retention:** FAL.ai retains uploaded files and generated content for a minimum of 7 days before deletion. WaveSpeed AI automatically deletes all user-generated content after 7 days. Refer to their respective policies for further details: [FAL.ai](https://fal.ai/privacy), [WaveSpeed AI](https://wavespeed.ai/docs/data-retention-policy).
*   **Task history:** The Application stores metadata about your generation tasks (such as prompts, settings, and links to generated videos) in its database as part of your account.
*   **Account data:** We retain your account data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter.
*   **Deletion requests:** To request deletion of your data, contact us at josephzhang.works@gmail.com.
