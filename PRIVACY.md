# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Image to Video app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Jiafu Zhang (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

**Information We Collect**

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

*   Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
*   The time and date of your usage, the time spent on the Application
*   The operating system you use on your mobile device

The Application does not gather information about the location of your mobile device.

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information. The information that the Service Provider requests will be retained and used as described in this privacy policy.

**How We Use Your Information**

The core functionality of the Application involves generating AI-powered videos from your photos, videos, audio, and text prompts. When you use these features, the following data is sent to third-party AI service providers for processing:

*   Images you select for video generation (which may contain human faces)
*   Videos you upload for enhancement or reference
*   Audio files you provide for avatar or talking video features
*   Text prompts you enter and technical parameters (such as resolution and duration settings)

Your content is used solely for the purpose of generating AI videos, enhancing video quality, or creating audio as requested by you. Your content is never used for facial recognition, user identification, authentication, advertising, or any purpose other than the video generation you initiate.

**Face Data**

Certain features of the Application involve photos that may contain human faces (such as image-to-video generation, avatar creation, or face-driven animation). This section explains how face data is handled:

*   **What face data is collected:** When you upload photos containing human faces, those photos are transmitted to our third-party AI service providers for processing. The Application does not perform local facial recognition, extract facial geometry, create facial feature maps, or generate biometric identifiers from your photos.
*   **How face data is used:** Face data in your uploaded photos is used solely for generating AI-powered videos as requested by you, including animating faces, creating talking avatars, and producing face-driven video content.
*   **Third-party sharing:** Photos containing face data are transmitted only to FAL.ai and WaveSpeed AI for AI video generation processing. No face data is sold, shared with, or disclosed to any other third parties, except as required by law (see "Data Disclosure" below).
*   **No biometric data:** The Application does not collect, create, or store biometric identifiers or biometric information as defined under applicable biometric privacy laws. No facial geometry, faceprint, or facial recognition template is extracted from your photos at any point.

**How We Share Your Information**

The Application uses the following third-party services:

*   [FAL.ai](https://fal.ai/privacy) — AI video and image generation
*   [WaveSpeed AI](https://wavespeed.ai/static/privacy) — AI video generation
*   [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) — Subscription and purchase management

Aggregated and anonymized data may periodically be transmitted to external services to help improve the Application.

**Data Retention and Storage**

*   **Your uploaded photos:** The Application does not store your uploaded photos on its own servers. Photos are uploaded directly to the AI service providers for processing and are temporarily hosted on their infrastructure.
*   **AI provider retention:** FAL.ai retains uploaded files and generated content for a minimum of 7 days before deletion. WaveSpeed AI automatically deletes all user-generated content after 7 days. Please refer to their respective policies for further details: [FAL.ai](https://fal.ai/privacy), [WaveSpeed AI](https://wavespeed.ai/docs/data-retention-policy).
*   **Task history:** Generated video URLs and task metadata (prompts, settings) are stored in the Application's database as part of your account.
*   **Account data:** The Service Provider will retain user-provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter.
*   **Deletion requests:** If you would like your data deleted, please contact josephzhang.works@gmail.com. You may also delete your generated content at any time within the Application.

**Data Disclosure**

The Service Provider may disclose your information:

*   As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process;
*   When they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
*   With trusted service providers who work on their behalf and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

If the Service Provider discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal information, they will immediately delete it. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact josephzhang.works@gmail.com.
